Help Menu


System

Click on Register button, and choose whether Employee or Customer. After being directed to the correct form, fill in required information, and submit to register an account.

Use the ID and correct password to login to the system, and system will direct to the corresponding Home Page based on the role of the login user.

Every 30 minute the system will check all open auction, and if an auction with past closed time is still open, the system will close the auction.

Customer Representative

Employee Home Page displays Personal Information of the login Customer Representative. To edit information, edit the corresponding field and click the Edit button at the bottom.

Click on Record Sale on the sidebar, and direct to Sales Record page. Input the Auction ID in the Search Auction field, and click Search button. If the auction with given Auction ID exists, is closed, and has been bidded on, then the sale record would appear displaying all information; otherwise, the error “No sales record for this auction” is displayed.

Click on Mailing List on the sidebar, and direct to Mailing List page. The mailing information of all existing customers would all be automatically generated and displayed.

Click on Item Suggestion on the sidebar, and direct to Item Suggestion Page. Input the Customer ID in Search Customer, and click Search button to find the customer to generate the item suggestions for. If the customer does exist, the top 5 item suggestions with the item information would be displayed; otherwise, the error “Customer Not Found” is displayed.

Click on Receipt on the sidebar, and direct to Receipt page. Input Auction ID in Search Auction field, click the Search button to find the auction to generate receipt for. If the auction does exist and has receipt, the receipt information would be displayed; otherwise, the error “No receipt for this auction” is displayed.

Click on New Customer under Customers under the sidebar, and direct to the Customer Registration form. Input all required information and submit, the customer will be added.

Click on Find Customer under Customers on the sidebar, and direct to Customer Management page. Input Customer ID in Search Customer field, click the Search button to find the customer to edit. If the customer does exist, customer information is displayed; otherwise, the error “No receipt for this auction” is displayed. To update customer, click on Edit button and direct to Customer Edit Page. Edit information in the fields and click Edit to update customer information. To delete customer, simply click Delete button.

Click on Employee Info on the sidebar, and direct to Employee Info page. All employee information will be automatically generated and displayed in a table.
